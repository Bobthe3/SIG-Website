About Us

Learn more about UCSD's Sustainable Investment Group and our mission

Our Mission

The Sustainable Investment Group at UC San Diego is dedicated to educating students about responsible investing and sustainable finance. We believe that financial decisions can drive positive environmental and social impact while generating competitive returns.

Our organization serves as a bridge between academic learning and practical application in the rapidly growing field of sustainable finance, preparing students for careers that align profit with purpose.

Our Values

Sustainability

We prioritize long-term environmental and social impact in all investment considerations and organizational decisions.

Innovation

We embrace new approaches and technologies that advance sustainable finance and responsible investing practices.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in our analysis, recommendations, and organizational practices.

Collaboration

We foster an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives contribute to better investment decisions.

What We Offer

Educational Workshops

Regular workshops covering ESG analysis, impact investing, green bonds, and sustainable finance fundamentals.

Industry Connections

Networking opportunities with professionals in sustainable finance, impact investing, and ESG research.

Research Projects

Hands-on experience analyzing sustainable investment opportunities and creating research reports.

Professional Development

Resume reviews, interview preparation, and career guidance for sustainable finance roles.

Guest Speakers

Regular presentations from industry leaders, alumni, and sustainable finance experts.

Portfolio Competitions

Competitive events where members create and present sustainable investment portfolios.

Our History

2021

Foundation

2022

First Industry Panel

Hosted our first industry panel featuring sustainable finance professionals from leading firms.

2023

Research Initiative

Launched our research initiative, producing quarterly reports on sustainable investment trends.

2024

Alumni Network

Established our alumni network with graduates now working at top sustainable finance firms.
