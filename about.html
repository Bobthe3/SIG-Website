What is SIG?

The Sustainable Investment Group at UC San Diego (SIG) is a student-run growth investment organization that conducts research and invests in stocks of companies in industry sectors conducive to sustainability to achieve high returns. We teach students various skills such as modeling, researching, industry analysis, and presentation both broadly and through the unique lens of sustainability, giving students experience in sustainability, finance, and counseling.

Our Values

Sustainability

We prioritize long-term environmental and social impact in all investment considerations and organizational decisions.

Innovation

We embrace new approaches and technologies that advance sustainable finance and responsible investing practices.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in our analysis, recommendations, and organizational practices.

Collaboration

We foster an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives contribute to better investment decisions.

Our Committees

Equity Research

Our Equity Research team conducts in-depth analysis of publicly traded companies, utilizing advanced valuation techniques and ESG criteria to identify sustainable investment opportunities that balance financial returns with environmental and social impact.

Sustainability

The Sustainability committee specializes in ESG research and analysis, evaluating companies' environmental, social, and governance practices. We integrate sustainability metrics into investment decisions, helping identify companies with strong long-term impact potential while avoiding greenwashing.

Quantitative Analysis

Our Quant team develops and applies statistical models and algorithms to forecast market trends and understand company financials. Using Python and advanced modeling techniques like ARCH, GARCH, and ESG-CAPM, we bring data-driven insights to sustainable investment decisions.

Marketing

This committee manages SIG's brand presence and member experience, organizing internal events, coordinating external partnerships, and handling social media outreach. They create opportunities for professional networking and foster a strong organizational culture that connects members across all committees.

Research & Development

The R&D committee focuses on strategic initiatives including fundraising, developing investment frameworks, and building portfolio optimization tools. They create the infrastructure and methodologies that enable SIG to integrate ESG considerations into real-world investment decision-making.
